Central Valley Pottery Studio

Brief Introduction

Central Valley Pottery Studio combines a pottery teaching studio with a gallery showcasing the works of many Central Valley potters. Classes in wheelthrowing, handbuilding, and tile making are offered to adults in 8-10 week sessions. Children can participate in monthly parent/child classes and summer camps. Workshops are offered by local and nationally-known potters. Private lessons can also be arranged. The gallery includes functional, decorative stoneware and earthenware at surprisingly reasonable prices.
